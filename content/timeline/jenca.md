## Jenca Ltd

Jenca was a Not For Profit company offering a PaaS/IaaS product on which Architecture, Engineering and Construction (AEC) web application developers can easily market and run their applications at a global scale. We are a community project that is 100% open source. Jenca has been featured in <a href="http://www.bimplus.co.uk/news/google-ins4pires-n7ew-bim-app-platform-j7enca/" target="blank">BIM+ magazine</a> and is partnered with other cutting edge AEC web applications like the <a href="http://bimserver.org/" target="blank">BIMserver</a>, <a href="http://3drepo.org/" target="blank">3D Repo</a> and <a href="http://duraark.eu/" target="blank">Duraark</a>.</p>
