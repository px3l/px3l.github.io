CTO at BIMLauncher Ltd

BIMLauncher Ltd develops and runs integrations between cloud software. The platform offers highly configurable and customisable workflows to be built between software for construction projects which enable data synchronization and migrations.

From the digitilastion of the AEC industries emerged a new and important concept: the Common Data Environment (CDE). Many project management solutions were developed each offering a set of tools and claiming to act as the CDE but the reality is that across a large construction project, teams of different disciplines, geographic locations and even different stages of the project lifecycle, choose their own digital tools. This leaves a broken CDE as data is locked in silos and has to be manually transferred between teams chosen solution.

BIMLauncher allows data to be moved from one software to another, so that tools can be used side by side (data synchronization) or a new tool introduced with ease (data migration). BIMLauncher solves the disconnect in the CDE and allows best of breed tools to be used across a whole construction project seamlessly.

As CTO it is my responsibility to deliver on customer requirements and features by managing the development and operations of our software. The platform is built in modern micro-service based web technologies (primarily written in nodeJS) and runs on AWS. To successfully meet deadlines and deliver the best version of our software, a deep understanding of our system architecture, functionality and operations is required.
