Co-founder & CTO at Jenca Ltd

Building on the idea of an open platform for building data, Jenca was an attempt to help democratize the building process by allowing non-technical workers found in the AEC industries to engage with open technologies.

The premise was a platform which allowed users to run compile applications at scale. These applications were deployed as collections, which we called Projects.

The platform was based on a NodeJS architecture running on Docker and Kubernetes.

Jenca has been featured in BIM+ magazine and partnered with other cutting edge AEC web applications like the BIMserver, 3D Repo and Duraark.

We secured funding through a business accelerator in London, however the project required a significant R&D stage in order to explore how these applications would work together and the investors were not willing to engage further until a solution was discovered.
